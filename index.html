Who We Are

SCHNAERO is committed to revolutionizing the world of electric vehicles by providing quality, cost-effective solutions. Our dedication lies in innovation, management consulting, and cutting-edge research. TAG Automotive is a comprehensive organisation aimed at creating, innovating and delivering futuristic modes of transportation throughout the masses. It aims at providing mobility from a personal platform to a collective platform, innovating at every step to bring forth a world with negligible emissions through these transport systems. The organisation strives to create an environment that is user centric and blends with the nature in the most energy efficient way possible.

Our team of experts continuously strives to redefine the electric automobile industry, focusing on sustainability and eco-friendliness. By integrating the latest technologies, we aim to provide our customers with unmatched experiences and value for their investment. We at TAG believe in offering our customers revolutionary design in terms of aesthetics, ergonomics, safety and functionalities in our vehicles. Conceptual design illuminates a new road to distinctive design elements and hence, we strive to pitch our thinking to the farthest point possible in order to break the conventions in automotive design. Technological advancements go in tandem with conceptualisation and engineering to surpass the current trends, so that our vehicles provide you with luxury and peace of mind, transforming your world into a mesmerising experience.
